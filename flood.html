Floods

What Is It?

A flood is a large amount of water that ends up on land, resulting in the streets and buildings being submerged underwater. Flash floods are dangerous floods that are able to destroy buildings with their strong currents.

How Does It Happen?

Floods occur from several different factors, including:

  • The aftermath of a tsunami (a large ocean wave caused by an earthquake)
  • Extremly heavy rainfall (which can cause small rivers and streams to overflow)
  • Dam failures
  • Snowmelts
  • When the ground cannot absorb the amount of rain that falls

How Can I Prepare?

  • Monitor official flood watch sites
  • Get to higher ground immediately!
  • Avoid escaping the floods by driving through the water. Floods arrive quickly!
